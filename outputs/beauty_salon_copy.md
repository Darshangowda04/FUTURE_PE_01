Our signature facial is a customized 60-minute treatment combining advanced skincare technology with relaxing hand massage. It's designed to address your specific skin concerns—whether acne, sensitivity, aging, or dehydration—leaving your skin glowing and hydrated.

**4 Key Benefits:**

1. **Deep Hydration & Glow**
   Infused with premium serums and professional-grade products, your skin receives nourishment at the deepest level, revealing a radiant complexion immediately.

2. **Fine Line Reduction**
   Our specialized techniques and high-tech equipment reduce the appearance of wrinkles, promoting natural collagen production for long-term skin improvement.

3. **Clear, Healthy Skin**
   Perfect for acne-prone or sensitive skin. We use customized products and gentle techniques to clear congestion while maintaining your skin's natural balance.

4. **Stress Relief & Relaxation**
   Beyond skin benefits, you'll experience pure tranquility. Our therapists combine skincare with therapeutic massage to melt away stress and tension.

**How It Works:**
1. **Consultation (5 min)** - We assess your skin type and concerns

4. **Mask & Therapy (10 min)** - Healing mask while you relax
5. **Finish (5 min)** - Moisturize and protect your skin

**FAQ:**

### Service 2: LUXURY HAIR TREATMENTS

**Transform Your Hair, Transform Your Confidence**
Our professional hair treatments combine nourishing formulas with expert technique to repair damage, enhance shine, and restore your hair's natural beauty. Perfect for color-treated, curly, or damaged hair.

**4 Key Benefits:**

1. **Intense Repair & Restoration**
   Damaged, brittle hair gets a second chance. Our treatments penetrate deep to repair split ends and restore elasticity, making your hair feel silky and strong.

2. **Vibrant Color That Lasts**
   If you color-treat your hair, our specialized treatments lock in color and prevent fading, keeping your shade vibrant for weeks longer.

3. **Frizz Control & Smooth Shine**
   Say goodbye to frizz! Our smoothing treatments tame unruly hair while adding mirror-like shine that turns heads.

4. **Time-Saving Hair Health**
   One treatment every 2-4 weeks keeps your hair in salon-fresh condition, reducing the need for frequent styling and touch-ups.

**The Process:**
1. **Hair Analysis** - We assess damage level and hair goals
2. **Pre-Treatment Rinse** - Remove buildup and prepare hair
3. **Custom Treatment Application** - Deep conditioning formula applied
4. **Heat Setting (15-20 min)** - Heat activates treatment benefits
5. **Final Rinse & Style** - Reveal your transformed hair

**FAQ:**
- **How long do results last?** - 4-6 weeks; prolong with at-home products
- **Will it damage my hair?** - No! Our treatments strengthen and repair
- **Best for colored hair?** - Absolutely; all treatments are color-safe
